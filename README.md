# Enterprise Products Partners

Enterprise Products Partners L.P. is one of the largest publicly traded partnerships and a leading North American provider of midstream energy services. The company provides natural gas and crude oil pipeline transportation, natural gas processing, NGL fractionation, storage, and marine transportation services.
